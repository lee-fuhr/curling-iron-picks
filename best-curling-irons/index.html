Our verdict

There’s no single “best” curling iron for everyone, but starting with the Conair InfinitiPRO gives you the most bang for your buck across a broad range of hair types. If you’re a pro or have stubborn hair, the BaByliss PRO or Hot Tools will serve you better. Budget shoppers will appreciate the Remington, and those chasing high-tech speed and low heat should consider the Dyson Airwrap. Whichever you choose, always use a heat protectant and match the barrel size to your desired curl, that’s the real secret to great results.
